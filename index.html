About Us

Enloe Math Honor Society is a local chapter of Mu Alpha Theta (National Math Honor Society). We strive to create an inclusive and welcoming math community with opportunities for students to challenge and broaden their skillset, while simultaneously encouraging students to participate in different math-related activities, mini-competitions, and even try out for the competitive team -- which will compete at prestigious math competitions such as Duke Math Meet and AMC.

Besides the competition aspect, we also have frequent meetings where students have the opportunity to interact with the Enloe community, are presented rigorous math seminars and lessons, can participate in math competitions for students of all levels, and, of course, have fun discussing math problems.

Meetings will be held every other week after school on Thursdays from 2:30-3:30. We hope to see you there!
